Background

I've pretty much been around computers my whole life. As a kid growing up, I spent most of my time on a computer, mainly playing games and surfing the web. On most days I would stay on for several hours at a time (until I got yelled at by my parents :D). I've always known ever since I was a kid, that I wanted to work with computers when I got older, even if I didn't know exactly what I wanted to do. It wasn't until the summer of my Junior year of high school however, that I decided to go into computer programming. One of my relatives (who is a programmer) first piqued my interest about programming while I was visiting him on vacation. I tried it out for myself and instantly fell in love with it! To this day, I have made all sorts of appications, games, and other such things for both school and just for fun.
